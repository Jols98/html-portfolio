The Best Albums According to Josh

My top 3 albums of all-time.


Somewhere City - Origami Angel

Emo masterpiece from a two piece group.


Madvillainy - Madvillain

Hip-Hop classic from the minds of rapper MF DOOM and producer extraordinaire Madlib.


The College Dropout - Kanye West

One of the best "freshman" albums to ever be released in my opinion.
